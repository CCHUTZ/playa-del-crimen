Welcome to the Crime Beach

Playa del Crimen is an educational project designed to teach OSINT (Open Source Intelligence), threat intelligence, and sociotechnical security through immersive, narrative-driven training scenarios. This is not your typical cybersecurity course. Here, you will learn to think like a threat actor, understand the human element of security, and build better defenses by exploring realistic, fictional case studies.

Our philosophy is simple: the best way to learn defense is to understand offense. Each scenario is a deep dive into the tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) used by real-world threat actors, presented in a safe and ethical educational environment.

Who Is This For?

This project is designed for cybersecurity professionals, intelligence analysts, red teamers, security educators, and business leaders who want to understand the real-world risks that organizations face in the digital age.

Playa del Crimen is a fictional place. Any resemblance to real people, places, or events is purely coincidental. All scenarios are for educational purposes only.
