# Team Structure: The Intelligence Cell

This operation will be conducted by a small, agile intelligence cell, with each member playing a critical role. This structure is based on real-world corporate and governmental intelligence teams.

## The Roles

- **[Threat Intelligence Lead](./threat_intel_lead.md):** The strategic mind of the operation.
- **[OSINT Analyst](./osint_analyst.md):** The master of open-source information.
- **[Social Engineering Analyst](./social_engineer.md):** The expert on human behavior.
- **[Data Correlation Specialist](./data_correlation.md):** The pattern-finder and dot-connector.
- **[Risk Impact Analyst](./risk_analyst.md):** The voice of caution and consequence.
