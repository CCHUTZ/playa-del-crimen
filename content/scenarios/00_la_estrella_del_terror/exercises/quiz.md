# Quiz: La Estrella del Terror

**1. What is a sociotechnical attack surface?**

   a) The physical security of a building
   b) The combination of human, social, and technical vulnerabilities in an organization
   c) The firewall configuration of a network
   d) The encryption protocols used by a system

**2. What is the primary goal of a sociotechnical intelligence operation?**

   a) To steal money
   b) To cause technical damage
   c) To gain strategic information dominance
   d) To install malware

**3. What is pretexting?**

   a) A type of malware
   b) A method of encrypting data
   c) The act of creating a believable story to manipulate someone
   d) A type of firewall

**4. What is a watering hole attack?**

   a) An attack that targets a specific individual
   b) An attack that targets a specific website or online resource that is trusted by the target
   c) An attack that targets a physical water source
   d) An attack that targets a specific type of software

**5. What is the most important lesson to be learned from this scenario?**

   a) Technology is the most important aspect of cybersecurity
   b) Cybersecurity starts with culture and human behavior
   c) The only way to be secure is to disconnect from the internet
   d) All of the above

---

**Answers:** 1. (b), 2. (c), 3. (c), 4. (b), 5. (b)
